Hindustan WMS Smart Waste Management Software System

The powerful cloud-based platform helps customers to configure, monitor and plan everyday waste management activities and belongs to one of the best waste management software on the market these days. The customer sees all the bins on a digital map and via street view including capacity, waste type, last measurement, GPS location, and collection schedule or pick recognition.

You built detailed bin inventory, optimize bin location (capacity, cost), record maintenance evidence. For bins with Smart sensors, you can to set up and configure sensors, monitor real-time fill level, see predictions, plan collection routes, see pick reports and deal with reports from citizens.

Waste management software system is available in 10 languages and you can integrate smart waste data to your current IS via API.

01

Detailed bin inventory

All monitored bins and stands can be seen on the map , and you can visit them at any time via the Street View feature from Google. Bins or stands are visible on the map as green, orange or red circles. The number in the circle represents last measurement (%).
 You can see bin details in the Dashboard –capacity, waste type, last measurement, GPS location and collection schedule or pick recognition.

02

Real-time bin monitoring

The Dashboard displays real-time data on fill-levels of bins monitored by smart sensors.
 In addition to the % of fill-level, based on the historical data, the tool predicts when the bin will become full, one of the functionalities that are not included even in the best waste management softwares. Sensors recognize picks as well; so you can check when the bin was last collected.
 With real-time data and predictions, you can eliminate the overflowing bins and stop collecting half-empty ones.

 

03

Expensive bins

We help you identify bins that drive up your collection costs. The tool calculates a rating for each bin in terms of collection costs. The tool considers the average distance depo-bin-discharge in the area. The tool assigns bin a rating (1-10) and calculates distance from depo-bin discharge.
 You can relocate depo or discharge. You can adjust pricing based on the rating.

04

Understand filling cycles

What is the filling cycle for the bin? Find a bin on the map or in the dashboard, and access a filling graph to see its filling cycles and picks over the last few weeks.
 If it gets full very fast, you might consider increasing capacity to save collection costs.

05

Do not miss a pick

How many bins did you miss last week? For periodically picked bins, we provide Pick evaluation. The tool records picks (sensor) and compares them to the schedule.
 You can immediately identify any missed, or off-schedule picks.

06

Adjust bin distribution

Ensure the most optimal distribution of bins. Identify areas with either dense or sparse bin distribution . Make sure all trash types are represented within a stand.
Based on the historical data, you can adjust bin capacity or location where necessary.

07

Eliminate unefficient picks

Eliminate the collection of half-empty bins. The sensors recognize picks. By using real-time data on fill-levels and pick recognition , we can show you how full the bins you collect are .
The report shows how full the bin was when picked. You immediately see any inefficient picks below 80% full.

08

Plan waste collection routes

The tool semi-automates waste collection route planning. Based on current bin fill-levels and predictions of reaching full capacity, you are ready to respond and schedule waste collection.
You can compare planned vs. executed routes to identify any inconsistencies.

09

Calculate costs

The waste management software uses several variables to calculate waste collection costs, and helps you reduce them. Bellow the map, you can see Costs, Costs/m3, Cost/kg, Duration, Length km, Volume m3 and Weight kg for each route.
In addition to your bins, you can also set up collection vehicles, depots, discharges, and set their related costs.

10

Solve reports

Introduce Citizen app to citizens. It allows them to report any issues with bins via a mobile app. You can resolve these issues in the Dashboard.
The reports include bin identification, GPS location and citizens can even attach photos. Using the app is faster and easier than reporting via email or social media.

Mobile App for drivers and operators

We understand that waste collection professionals spend a lot of time outside and welcome anything that makes their jobs easier. Therefore we have developed the Hindustan WMS mobile application, which is tailored to their specific needs and enables quick and convenient usage.

Fire alarm and other warnings

Sensors send data on battery status and have a built-in fire alarm . You can see all the warnings on the Dashboard and/or send SMS or Email notifications to the responsible people.

Pickup Recognition

Sensors have built-in motion detection and recognizes when was the bin emptied. Dashboard provides both data-driven and sensor-driven pickup (emptying) recognition.

Bin Management

Sensorless Bin Management is a smart, enterprise-grade bin management solution for cities and businesses to digitalize bin inventories, cost-efficiently manage bin distribution and make data-driven decisions. It includes all the features of the Smart waste management software system mentioned above without monitoring (real-time data on fill-level).
